The Best Movies According to Dez

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ex Machina (2014)

A sleek, cerebral thriller that delves into the ethics and implications of creating conscious machines. The story follows a programmer who evaluates the human qualities of an advanced humanoid AI, raising questions about control, autonomy, and the nature of intelligence.

Her (2013)

This film takes a softer, more introspective look at AI. It follows a man who develops a deep, emotional relationship with an advanced operating system. "Her" examines the evolution of human relationships in a technology-saturated world, questioning what it means to love and be loved by a non-human entity.

A.I. Artificial Intelligence (2001)

Directed with a visionary blend of Spielberg’s storytelling and Kubrick’s conceptual groundwork, this film presents a futuristic tale of a childlike android designed to feel love. It explores themes of identity, the quest for belonging, and the moral dilemmas surrounding artificial sentience.
